Privacy Policy for Painless Touch Care

Last updated:12th December, 2025

Painless Touch Care (“we”, “our”, or “the app”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

a. Personal Information: Name, email, phone number, profile details, and appointment information.

b. Health-Related Information: Any health notes you voluntarily provide.

c. Device Information: IP address, device model, operating system, usage data, crash logs.

d. Permissions: Camera, storage, notifications (only if you grant access).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide app services, manage appointments, send reminders, improve performance, and maintain security.

3. Sharing of Information

We only share data with healthcare partners you select, service providers who support app operation, or when required by law. We do not sell your data.

4. Data Security

We use encryption and security practices to protect your information but cannot guarantee absolute protection.

5. Data Retention

We retain data as long as needed to operate the app or comply with legal obligations. You may request account deletion.

6. Children’s Privacy

The app is not intended for children under 13. If such data is found, it will be deleted.

7. Your Rights

You may request access, edits, deletion, or withdrawal of permissions at any time.

8. Third-Party Services

We may use services like Google Firebase or analytics tools. These have their own privacy policies.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Updates will appear on this page.
